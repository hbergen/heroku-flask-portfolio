About me

I'm a data scientist living in Seattle, Washington, and I currently work at Nordstrom, where I apply my skills in digital marketing.

  • 2002 to 2009: Absorbing all the things
    With minimal background in science or mathematics from high school, I decided that mathematics was a challenge I wanted to pursue in my undergraduate studies and switched my major from English to Mathematics. I earned a B.S. in Mathematics from Columbia College in 2006, a small private teaching college in my hometown, and a M.S. in Applied Mathematics from the University of Missouri in 2009.
  • 2009 to 2015: Professional data diving
    After I completed my education, I spent the next few years gaining foundational experience working with data within a wide range of industries. I have applied my curiosity and attention to detail in industries ranging from property insurance, real estate assessment, biochemical defense, transportation safety research, and cancer research.
  • 2015 to now: shift to analytics
    During the summer of 2015 I joined Nordstrom to pursue a career in analytics and data science for the opportunity to increase my exposure to statistics applied in more of a business setting. In my current role as a Data Scientist at Nordstrom, I lead analyses from start to finish in support of marketing while leveraging new tools, refining documentation, and automating processes wherever possible.
